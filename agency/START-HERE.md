## WHAT KILLS FLOW (Don't Do This)

❌ Waiting for "perfect" before moving forward
❌ Requiring every character fully modeled before approval
❌ Making character creation sequential (wait for approval before starting next)
❌ Trying to predict where bottleneck is (it's invisible until it happens)
❌ Delaying platform analysis (do it NOW, informs everything)

---

## WHAT MAKES FLOW (Do This)

✅ Get SOMETHING through the whole system (even imperfect)
✅ Parallel processing (art dept creating while Joe approves while CX generates)
✅ Iterate based on feedback, not perfection
✅ Watch where it breaks, then fix that
✅ Platform analysis first (tells you what to build)
