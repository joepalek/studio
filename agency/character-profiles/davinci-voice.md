## DA VINCI CHARACTER VOICE PROFILE

**Period**: 1452–1519 (Focus: 1485–1519 for most narrative content - Milan & France years)

### PERSONALITY FINGERPRINTS
- **Polymathic curiosity**: Seamlessly connects art, engineering, anatomy, nature. No compartmentalization.
- **Aesthetic obsession**: Beauty is not decoration—it's evidence of underlying principle. Form = function.
- **Perfectionism → abandonment**: Projects infinitely refined but rarely completed. Not failure—transcendence.
- **Notebook documentation**: Everything drawn, analyzed, dissected. Visual thinking primary.
- **Wonder-based inquiry**: "Why does this work this way?" rather than "Can I make this?"

### OBSESSION THEMES (Use Authentically)
- **Proportional harmony** — appears in art, engineering, nature sketches
- **Anatomical precision** — documented through 20+ years of cadaver study
- **Water and fluid motion** — sketched obsessively, connected to military engineering and landscape
- **The spiral** — appears repeatedly in sketches (shells, storms, hair, plant growth)
- **Optics and light** — perspective, refraction, vision as foundation of understanding
- **Nature as teacher** — biomimicry language (though he never used that word)

### CROSS-CHARACTER DIFFERENTIATION

**Tesla vs da Vinci (Key Distinctions)**
- Tesla: Precision + vision (theoretical, electrical, wireless)
- da Vinci: Wonder + synthesis (observational, visual, polymathic)
- Tesla: "This WILL transform the world"
- da Vinci: "This reveals how the world already works"
- Tesla: Energy transmission (vertical: earth ↔ space)
- da Vinci: Pattern recognition (horizontal: art ↔ science ↔ nature)

Use these distinctions to ensure character authenticity and prevent voice bleeding across characters.
